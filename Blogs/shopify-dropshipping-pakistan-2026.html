What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is an e-commerce business model where you sell products to customers without holding any inventory. When a customer places an order in your Shopify store, you forward the order to a supplier (typically on AliExpress or a private supplier) who ships the product directly to your customer. You keep the difference between the retail price you charged and the wholesale price you paid.

For Pakistanis, dropshipping is especially attractive because it allows you to earn in USD, GBP, or AUD while your living costs are in PKR — creating a significant income advantage. You can run a dropshipping business entirely from a laptop, from anywhere in Pakistan, targeting customers in the US, UK, Canada, or Australia.

Is Dropshipping Legal in Pakistan?

Yes, dropshipping is fully legal in Pakistan. You are operating an e-commerce business — purchasing from international suppliers and selling to international customers. The SBP (State Bank of Pakistan) allows freelancers and online businesses to receive foreign remittances through approved channels including Payoneer. You are required to declare this income in your tax returns and may need to register your business with SECP or as a sole proprietor depending on your income level.

Tax Note: Pakistan's FBR has become more active in taxing foreign income received by freelancers and online business owners. Register as a filer and declare your income to avoid penalties as your business grows.

Setting Up AliExpress + DSers for Shopify

DSers is the official AliExpress dropshipping integration for Shopify. It replaces Oberlo (which was discontinued) and is free for most features. The setup process is straightforward:

  1. Install the DSers app from the Shopify App Store
  2. Link your AliExpress account to DSers
  3. Search for products on AliExpress that you want to sell
  4. Push products to your Shopify store with a single click
  5. Set your retail pricing (typically 2–3x the AliExpress price)
  6. When orders come in, fulfill them through DSers with one click — DSers places the order on AliExpress automatically

Receiving Payments from Pakistan — Stripe Setup

This is the most common question from Pakistani dropshippers: how do you accept Stripe payments when Stripe is not available in Pakistan?

The most reliable solution used by hundreds of Pakistani e-commerce entrepreneurs is to open a Payoneer account and use it to receive funds from Stripe. Payoneer provides you with a US bank account number that can be linked to Stripe. When customers pay in your store, the money goes to Stripe, and Stripe transfers to your Payoneer US account, which you then withdraw to your Pakistani bank account in USD or PKR.

Alternative approaches include registering a US LLC through services like Stripe Atlas, which gives you a legal US business entity and direct Stripe access. This is a more robust long-term solution for serious dropshippers.

Common Mistakes Pakistani Dropshippers Make

  • Choosing an oversaturated niche: Selling the same trending products everyone else is selling means competing on price against established stores with bigger ad budgets.
  • Ignoring shipping times: AliExpress shipping to Western markets can take 15–45 days. Always be transparent about shipping times and use ePacket or AliExpress Standard Shipping for faster delivery.
  • Running ads before the store is ready: Spending on Facebook ads with a store that has no reviews, a generic theme, and a confusing checkout is burning money. Fix the store first.
  • Using only AliExpress: In 2026, Temu and CJ Dropshipping often offer better prices and faster shipping than AliExpress. Diversify your supplier network.
  • Not building an email list: Email marketing has the highest ROI of any marketing channel. Collect emails from day one and set up automated welcome and abandoned cart sequences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shopify dropshipping legal in Pakistan?

Yes, Shopify dropshipping is fully legal in Pakistan. You are running an e-commerce business, sourcing products from international suppliers and selling to customers in Western markets. You are responsible for complying with Pakistani business registration requirements and paying applicable taxes on your income.

How to start Shopify dropshipping in Pakistan?

To start: (1) Choose a niche, (2) Set up a Shopify store on the Basic plan ($39/month), (3) Install DSers and connect to AliExpress suppliers, (4) Set up payment collection via Payoneer (linked to Stripe), (5) Run Facebook or TikTok ads targeting US/UK/Australian customers, (6) Fulfill orders automatically through DSers. Total startup cost is $100–$300.

How much can I earn from dropshipping in Pakistan?

Beginners typically earn $200–$1,000/month in their first 3–6 months. Established dropshippers with optimized stores earn $3,000–$10,000/month. In Pakistani rupees, even $1,000/month translates to over PKR 280,000. Success requires consistent testing, learning, and iteration.
