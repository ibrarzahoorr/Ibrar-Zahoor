What is included in Shopify store setup?

A professional Shopify store setup includes theme installation and branding, product uploads with descriptions and images, payment gateway setup (Stripe, PayPal, or local gateways), shipping zone configuration, domain connection, legal policy pages, basic on-page SEO, and Google Analytics integration. How long does Shopify store setup take?

A standard Shopify store setup takes 3–5 business days. This assumes you provide product content, logo, and brand colors upfront. Larger catalogs (100+ products) or custom theme requirements extend the timeline to 7–14 days.
