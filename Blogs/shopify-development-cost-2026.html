What Affects Shopify Development Cost?

  • Number of products — 10 products vs 10,000 products requires different architecture
  • Custom design vs template — custom Liquid theme costs more than modifying an existing theme
  • App integrations — Klaviyo, ReCharge, Omnisend, Judge.me each add setup time
  • Payment gateways — multiple gateways (Stripe, PayPal, JazzCash) need individual setup
  • Multi-language / multi-currency — adds 20–30% to project cost
  • Shopify Plus features — Checkout Extensibility, B2B, Scripts require specialist knowledge
  • Timeline — rush delivery (under 48 hours) costs extra
