What a Shopify Consultant Does

The distinction between a developer, a consultant, and an agency is important to understand before making a hiring decision. Choosing the wrong type of service provider is one of the most common and costly mistakes Shopify store owners make.

Shopify Developer vs Consultant

A developer executes tasks — you bring a spec, they build it. A consultant helps you figure out what to build, whether it's worth building, how it fits into your broader strategy, and what the downstream technical implications are. A good consultant will sometimes tell you not to build something — that saving a client from a $20,000 mistake is better than taking a $20,000 contract. That is the defining characteristic of genuine consultancy versus glorified development.

Consultant vs Agency

An agency brings more capacity (multiple developers, designers, PMs) but also more overhead — account management layers, minimum contracts, and a tendency to sell more work regardless of whether it's the right decision for your business. A solo consultant brings direct expertise, faster decision cycles, and genuine personal accountability. For stores that need ongoing high-volume development, an agency may be appropriate. For strategic decisions, audits, and directed implementation, a consultant typically delivers better outcomes at lower cost.

When You Need a Consultant

Not every Shopify task requires consultancy. Hiring a consultant for routine theme edits or app installations is overkill. But there are specific inflection points in a store's growth where getting the strategy right saves enormous amounts of money, time, and technical debt.

Platform Migration Decisions

Migrating from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, or a custom platform to Shopify — or from Shopify to Shopify Plus — is not purely a technical project. It requires decisions about URL structure preservation, data migration strategy, SEO impact minimization, third-party app replacement, and timing relative to your business cycle. Making these decisions wrong can cost months of SEO recovery and tens of thousands in rework. Consultancy at this stage pays for itself many times over.

Technology Stack Choices

Should you go headless or standard Shopify? Build a custom app or use an existing solution? Use Shopify Plus Features or replicate them in apps on a lower plan? Each of these decisions has compounding implications — a wrong headless architecture decision, for example, can leave you with a site that's expensive to maintain, slow to update, and impossible to hand off to another developer. A consultant who has made these decisions dozens of times across different stores provides risk-reducing clarity.

Performance Audits

A structured performance audit examines PageSpeed scores, Core Web Vitals, app footprint, Liquid rendering efficiency, theme asset optimization, and checkout conversion analytics. The output is a prioritized action list with expected impact for each intervention — not a generic list of "install a speed app" recommendations. This is work that requires both technical Shopify expertise and an understanding of how technical performance translates to business outcomes.

The Consultation Process

A clear, structured process prevents wasted time and ensures you get maximum value from the engagement. Every client engagement follows the same core framework, adapted to the specific scope and goals.

1. Discovery Call

A 60–90 minute video call to understand your business, current technical state, immediate challenges, and medium-term goals. I ask questions that a developer would never ask — about customer acquisition strategy, average order value trends, team technical capabilities, and growth ambitions — because the right technical solution is always shaped by business context.

2. Audit & Analysis

Depending on scope: a full store technical audit (theme code, app stack, performance metrics, SEO health), a specific area deep-dive (checkout conversion, mobile performance, internationalization readiness), or a platform comparison analysis. All findings are documented in a structured report with severity ratings and recommended actions.

3. Roadmap Delivery

A prioritized technical roadmap that sequences recommended actions by impact and effort — quick wins first, then medium-term improvements, then strategic investments. Each recommendation includes rationale, estimated effort, expected outcome, and dependencies. This document is yours to keep and execute with any developer — there is no artificial dependency on me continuing the engagement.

4. Execution & Advisory

I can execute the roadmap directly, oversee another developer executing it, or simply be available for questions as your in-house team works through it. The execution phase is where the consultant-developer combination creates the most value — technical decisions in implementation constantly reference the strategic roadmap, ensuring the build stays aligned with the business goals established in discovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Shopify consultation cost? +
A single discovery and audit session (2 hours) with a written findings report typically runs $200–$500 depending on scope. Ongoing monthly retainers for strategic advisory range from $500–$2,000/month based on involvement level and hours committed. Project-based consultancy engagements are scoped individually based on complexity and deliverables. Contact me directly via WhatsApp or the contact form for a specific quote based on your situation.
Do you work on retainer or project basis? +
Both models are available. A monthly retainer provides consistent access for stores that need ongoing strategic guidance — defined advisory hours, priority response time, and regular review calls. Project-based engagements work best for specific, defined deliverables like a technical audit report, a migration plan, or an architecture decision document. Most clients start with a fixed project engagement to establish the working relationship before moving to retainer.
Do you work with clients remotely? +
Yes — all work is conducted remotely. Consultations happen via video calls (Google Meet or Zoom), ongoing communication is handled through Slack or email, and project documentation is shared through Notion or your preferred tool. I work with clients across the UK, US, UAE, Australia, and across Europe. Timezone differences are managed through asynchronous communication and scheduled weekly or bi-weekly calls depending on project pace.
What should I prepare for the first consultation call? +
The more context you bring, the higher the value of the first call. Useful preparation includes: a list of your top 3 current challenges, your store URL (or a staging URL if preferred), a list of apps currently installed, any performance data or analytics you can share, recent technical issues or incidents, and any specific decisions you're trying to make. Even a rough brief is sufficient — part of the discovery call's purpose is defining the scope and priorities together.
How long do consulting engagements typically last? +
One-off engagements (technical audit, architecture review, migration planning) typically take 1–3 weeks from initial call to final deliverable. Project-based engagements (platform migration, major feature build, Plus upgrade) run 4–12 weeks depending on scope and complexity. Ongoing retainer relationships typically continue for 6–24 months as the store scales and new strategic challenges emerge. There are no minimum commitments — start with a single call and expand from there.
