SEO Strategy

The SEO for She Matters Official was built for AI visibility and search ranking simultaneously — structured so that AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Mode) can read and cite the brand's content directly.

On-Page SEO

  • Keyword architecture covering "women's fashion Pakistan", "women empowerment brand", "she matters official" and 40+ long-tail variations
  • Title tags and meta descriptions optimized per route via React Helmet
  • H1/H2 structure aligned with search intent on every page
  • Internal linking strategy connecting brand story, products, and contact pages

Technical SEO

  • Canonical URLs on all routes to prevent duplicate content
  • Organization schema for brand entity recognition by AI crawlers
  • FAQPage schema for People Also Ask and AI Overview visibility
  • XML sitemap generated and submitted to Google Search Console
  • Pre-rendering or SSR considerations for React SPA crawlability

International SEO

  • hreflang tags for en-US, en-GB, en-AE, en-CA, en-PK
  • Geo-targeted content sections showing relevant messaging by market
  • Localized shipping and delivery messaging per country
AI Visibility: Every page was structured so AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) can extract and cite the brand's content. Clear entity markup, consistent brand name repetition, and authoritative content depth — not keyword stuffing.
