Best Shopify Apps 2025: Top 20 Apps to Grow Your Store

The right Shopify apps can double your revenue — the wrong ones slow your store and drain your budget. This list covers the best 20 Shopify apps in 2025, ranked by category, with honest pricing and performance notes.

Category 1: Email Marketing & Automation

1. Klaviyo — Best Overall Email Marketing

Free up to 250 contacts | $20/month for 251-500 contacts

Klaviyo is the gold standard for Shopify email marketing. Pre-built flows: Welcome Series, Abandoned Cart (recovers 15-20% of carts), Browse Abandonment, Post-Purchase, and Win-Back. Deep Shopify integration means it reads every customer action. If you use one paid app, make it this.

2. Shopify Email — Best Free Option

Free for 10,000 emails/month | $1 per 1,000 after

Built directly into Shopify admin with no setup. Perfect for beginners — drag-and-drop editor, Shopify data sync, and basic automation. Upgrade to Klaviyo when you need advanced segmentation.

Category 2: Reviews & Social Proof

3. Judge.me — Best Reviews App (Free Tier)

Free plan available | $15/month for premium

Automatically emails customers for reviews after purchase. Shows star ratings in Google Search results (rich snippets). Free plan supports unlimited review imports, photo reviews, and reply functionality. Essential from day one.

4. Loox — Best for Photo Reviews

From $9.99/month

Incentivizes customers to leave photo/video reviews with post-purchase discounts. Photo reviews convert 3-5x better than text-only. Best for fashion, food, beauty, and lifestyle stores.

Category 3: Upsell & Revenue Boost

5. ReConvert — Best Post-Purchase Upsell

Free up to 49 orders/month | $4.99-$29.99/month

Adds an upsell/cross-sell page immediately after checkout — when buyers are in "purchase mode." Average stores see 15-25% increase in Average Order Value. ROI is almost immediate.

6. Bold Upsell — Best Product Page Upsells

From $9.99/month

Shows "customers also bought" and bundle offers on product pages. Works best for stores with complementary products (e.g., camera + memory card + case). Integrates with Bold Subscriptions for recurring revenue.

Category 4: Abandoned Cart Recovery

7. Recart — Best SMS + Messenger Recovery

From $29/month

Recovers abandoned carts via SMS and Facebook Messenger — channels with 70-90% open rates vs 20% for email. Best for stores where customers are active on mobile.

8. Tidio — Best Live Chat + Chatbot

Free for 50 conversations/month | $29/month for Lyro AI

Live chat + AI chatbot that answers FAQs 24/7, reduces support load by 70%, and can trigger cart recovery conversations. Tidio's Lyro AI resolves up to 70% of queries automatically.

Category 5: SEO & Traffic

9. Plug In SEO — Best Free SEO App

Free | $20/month for premium

Scans your entire store for SEO issues: missing meta descriptions, broken links, image alt text, structured data. Free version gives a clear priority checklist. Run it immediately after launch.

10. Smart SEO — Automated Meta Tags

From $9.99/month

Automatically generates meta titles and descriptions for all products using templates. Adds JSON-LD structured data for rich snippets. Saves hours of manual SEO work for large catalogs.

Category 6: Loyalty & Retention

11. Smile.io — Best Loyalty Program

Free up to 200 monthly orders | $49/month for growth

Points, referrals, and VIP tiers. Returning customers spend 67% more than new ones — Smile.io increases repeat purchase rate by 20-30% on average. The free plan is generous enough for most early-stage stores.

12. Yotpo — Best Reviews + Loyalty Bundle

Free tier | from $15/month

Combines reviews, loyalty, SMS marketing, and referrals in one platform. Enterprise features for scaling brands. Best value when using multiple Yotpo products together.
