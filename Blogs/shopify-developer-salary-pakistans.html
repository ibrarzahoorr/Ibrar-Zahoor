5 Key Factors That Increase Your Salary

1. Experience Level (Base Factor)

  • 0-2 years: Base salary
  • 2-5 years: +40-60% increase
  • 5+ years: +80-120% increase
  • Expert (10+ years): +150-200% increase

2. City Location (15-25% Impact)

  • Karachi: +25% premium (tech hub, high demand)
  • Lahore: +20% premium (growing tech scene)
  • Islamabad: +15% premium (government & corporate)
  • Other cities: Base rates

3. Specialization (30-50% Premium)

  • Shopify Plus development: +40-60%
  • Custom app development: +30-50%
  • Headless commerce: +35-55%
  • Performance optimization: +20-35%

4. Certifications (15-35% Boost)

  • Shopify Partner Certification: +15-20%
  • Shopify Plus Certification: +25-35%
  • AWS/Cloud Certifications: +20-30%

5. Employment Type

  • Full-time: Stable, benefits, lower hourly equivalent
  • Freelance: 2-3x higher hourly rate, no benefits
  • Contract: Premium rates, project-based
  • Agency partnership: Regular work, competitive rates
