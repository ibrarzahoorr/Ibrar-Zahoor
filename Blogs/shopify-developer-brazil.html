Brazil-Specific E-commerce Features

Brazilian Tax Complexity (ICMS, NF-e)

Brazil has one of the world's most complex tax systems for e-commerce. Ibrar configures Shopify with correct ICMS rates by state, PIS/COFINS, and ISS where applicable. Nota Fiscal Eletrônica (NF-e) integration can be added through specialized Brazilian fiscal apps.

Installment Payment Culture

Brazilian consumers expect parcelamento — paying in 6, 10, or 12 interest-free installments. Ibrar implements this through Mercado Pago, PagSeguro, and other Brazilian gateways, dramatically increasing conversion rates for higher-ticket products.

Brazilian Logistics Partners

Shipping in Brazil requires integration with Correios, Jadlog, Total Express, and other carriers. Ibrar sets up real-time shipping calculations, tracking, and returns management optimized for Brazil's unique logistics landscape.
