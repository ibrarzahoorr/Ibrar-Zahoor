What is the Shopify Partner Program

The Shopify Partner Program is a free membership program designed for developers, designers, agencies, and consultants who build Shopify stores, apps, or provide services to Shopify merchants. Partners gain access to exclusive resources, revenue opportunities, and support tools.

Store Development

Build and customize Shopify stores for clients using development stores and exclusive partner tools.

App Development

Create and publish apps on the Shopify App Store with access to APIs and development resources.

Theme Development

Design and sell custom themes through the Theme Store or provide bespoke design services.

Agency Services

Offer comprehensive Shopify services including setup, design, marketing, and ongoing support.

Partner Program Types

Four Main Partner Categories:

  • Development Partners: Build stores, apps, and custom solutions for merchants
  • Theme Partners: Create and sell themes through the Shopify Theme Store
  • App Partners: Develop apps for the Shopify App Store ecosystem
  • Service Partners: Provide marketing, photography, copywriting, and other services

Program Eligibility

The Shopify Partner Program is open to:

  • Freelance Developers: Individual developers building Shopify solutions
  • Design Agencies: Teams providing design and development services
  • Marketing Agencies: Specialists in ecommerce marketing and optimization
  • App Developers: Software developers creating Shopify applications
  • Consultants: Ecommerce strategy and business consultants
  • Educators: Those teaching Shopify development or ecommerce

Development Store Access

One of the most valuable Shopify Partner benefits is unlimited access to development stores - fully functional Shopify stores that can be used for testing, client presentations, and development without subscription costs.

Development Store Features

Unlimited Stores

Create unlimited development stores with no monthly subscription fees or store limits.

Full Functionality

Access to all Shopify features including apps, themes, and administrative functions.

Password Protection

Stores are password-protected by default, perfect for client presentations and testing.

Easy Transfer

Seamlessly transfer completed stores to clients with all data and settings intact.

Development Store Benefits

1

Risk-Free Development

Build and test stores without financial risk. Experiment with different approaches and solutions before client presentation.

2

Client Demonstrations

Present working prototypes to clients for approval and feedback before final implementation and launch.

3

Training and Testing

Use development stores for team training, testing new apps, and experimenting with advanced features.

4

Portfolio Building

Create portfolio examples and case studies using development stores to showcase capabilities to prospects.

5

Seamless Handoff

Transfer completed stores to clients with single click, maintaining all customizations and data.

Development Store Limitations

Important Development Store Restrictions:

  • No Real Transactions: Cannot process real payments (test mode only)
  • Password Protected: Not publicly accessible without password
  • Transfer Required: Must transfer to client for public access
  • 50 Product Limit: Development stores limited to 50 products
  • No Custom Domain: Uses myshopify.com subdomain only
  • Limited Email: Cannot send actual customer emails

Best Practices for Development Stores

Maximize development store effectiveness:

  • Organized Naming: Use clear naming conventions for easy client identification
  • Client Collaboration: Share store passwords with clients for review and feedback
  • Version Control: Create backup development stores before major changes
  • Documentation: Document customizations and configurations for client handoff
  • Testing Protocol: Establish systematic testing process before client presentation
  • Transfer Preparation: Ensure all content and settings are finalized before transfer

Requirements & Application Process

Joining the Shopify Partner Program is free and straightforward, with minimal requirements. The application process focuses on understanding your background and intended use of partner resources.

Eligibility Requirements

Professional Intent

Demonstrate legitimate business purpose for building Shopify stores, apps, or providing services.

Valid Email Address

Professional email address for account verification and ongoing communications.

Business Information

Basic business details including company name, location, and service focus areas.

Terms Agreement

Accept Shopify Partner Terms of Service and program guidelines.

Application Process

1

Visit Partner Portal

Go to partners.shopify.com and click "Join for free" to start the application process.

2

Complete Application

Fill out basic information about yourself, your business, and your intended partner activities.

3

Email Verification

Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your provided email.

4

Account Approval

Most applications are approved immediately, with some requiring manual review within 24-48 hours.

5

Access Partner Dashboard

Once approved, access your partner dashboard to create development stores and track referrals.

Partner Responsibilities

Key Partner Obligations:

  • Quality Standards: Maintain high-quality work that reflects well on Shopify
  • Client Support: Provide adequate support and service to referred clients
  • Terms Compliance: Follow Shopify Partner Terms of Service and guidelines
  • Accurate Representation: Accurately represent your partnership status and capabilities
  • Ethical Practices: Maintain ethical business practices and avoid conflicts of interest
  • Continued Engagement: Remain active in the partner program to maintain benefits

Program Violations

Actions that can result in partner program suspension or termination:

  • Spam or Fraud: Engaging in spam, fraudulent referrals, or misleading practices
  • Policy Violations: Violating Shopify's acceptable use policies or terms of service
  • Poor Client Experience: Consistently providing poor service that reflects negatively on Shopify
  • Inactive Accounts: Extended periods of inactivity may result in account review
  • Competitive Activities: Promoting competing platforms while claiming partner status

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Shopify Partner Program and how does it work?
The Shopify Partner Program is a free program for developers, designers, and agencies who build Shopify stores and apps. Partners get development store access, revenue sharing on referrals, exclusive training resources, priority support, and marketing benefits to grow their Shopify-focused business.
What are the main benefits of being a Shopify Partner?
Key benefits include unlimited development stores, 20% recurring commission on referrals, exclusive training and certification programs, early access to new features, marketing co-op funds, partner directory listing, priority support channels, and revenue sharing opportunities.
How much can Shopify Partners earn through revenue sharing?
Partners earn 20% recurring commission on monthly subscription fees for referred merchants, plus one-time bounties. Development partners can earn $2,000+ per Plus referral, while app developers keep 80% of app revenue. Successful partners earn $10,000-50,000+ monthly.
How do clients benefit from working with Shopify Partners?
Clients get access to certified experts with proven knowledge, development store testing before launch, priority support for faster issue resolution, early access to new features, exclusive partner resources, and often better project outcomes compared to non-partner developers.
What are development stores and how do they work?
Development stores are fully functional Shopify stores that partners can create unlimited numbers of for free. They're password-protected, perfect for testing and client presentations, and can be seamlessly transferred to clients. They have limitations like 50 products and no real transactions.
How do I join the Shopify Partner Program?
Joining is free and simple. Visit partners.shopify.com, complete the application with basic business information, verify your email address, and most applications are approved immediately. You'll then have access to the partner dashboard and all program benefits.
What training and certification options are available?
Partners have access to Shopify Academy courses, developer documentation, regular webinars, community forums, and certification programs covering fundamentals, theme development, app development, Plus solutions, and marketing specializations with verified badges.
Can I be removed from the Shopify Partner Program?
Yes, partners can be removed for violations including spam/fraud, policy violations, consistently poor client experiences, extended inactivity, or promoting competing platforms. Maintaining quality standards and ethical practices ensures continued program membership.
