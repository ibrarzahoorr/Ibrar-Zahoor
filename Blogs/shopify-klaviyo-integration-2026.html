Why Klaviyo + Shopify is the Best Email Marketing Combination

Unlike generic email tools (Mailchimp, Constant Contact), Klaviyo is built around e-commerce event data. It natively understands Shopify's data model — orders, products, customers, carts — and uses that data to trigger hyper-personalized communications at scale. Key differentiators:

  • Real-time Shopify event syncing (viewed product, added to cart, placed order)
  • Predictive analytics (next order date, CLV, churn risk)
  • Built-in A/B testing for subject lines, send times, and content
  • Combined email + SMS from one platform
  • Deep segmentation on any Shopify data point

Step 1: Installing and Connecting Klaviyo to Shopify

  1. Create a Klaviyo account at klaviyo.com (free up to 500 contacts).
  2. In Klaviyo, go to Integrations → Shopify and enter your Shopify store URL.
  3. Click Connect to Shopify — this installs the Klaviyo app and grants the required permissions.
  4. Select the historical data to backfill: customers, orders, products.
  5. Add the Klaviyo on-site JavaScript snippet to your Shopify theme (via Theme Editor → Custom Code or the theme's app embed). This enables on-site tracking.
Ibrar Zahoor's Setup Tip: Always add the Klaviyo JavaScript snippet to your theme — not just the app integration. Without it, events like "Viewed Product" and "Active on Site" won't fire, limiting your ability to trigger browse abandonment and back-in-stock flows.

Step 3: The 5 Essential Klaviyo Flows for Shopify

Flows are automated email (and SMS) sequences triggered by customer behavior. Setting up the following five flows before sending any campaigns is Ibrar Zahoor's top recommendation — they generate revenue passively around the clock.

1. Abandoned Cart Flow

Triggered when a customer starts checkout but doesn't complete it. Typically the highest-revenue flow.

  • Email 1: 1 hour after abandonment — "You left something behind" (show product image, CTA)
  • Email 2: 24 hours — Add social proof (reviews, star ratings)
  • Email 3: 72 hours — Offer a time-limited discount (5-10%)

Expected recovery rate: 6-15% of abandoned checkouts.

2. Welcome Series Flow

Triggered when a new subscriber joins your list (via popup, checkout opt-in). Goal: build brand affinity and drive first purchase.

  • Email 1: Immediately — Welcome + brand story
  • Email 2: Day 2 — Your bestsellers / most popular collections
  • Email 3: Day 5 — Social proof (reviews, UGC) + discount offer
  • Email 4: Day 10 — Urgency reminder if no purchase yet

3. Post-Purchase Flow

Triggered after order placement. Goals: reinforce purchase decision, drive reviews, prompt second purchase.

  • Email 1: Immediately — Order confirmation + thank you
  • Email 2: Day 3 — Request a product review (post-delivery estimate)
  • Email 3: Day 14-21 — Cross-sell complementary products

4. Browse Abandonment Flow

Triggered when a subscriber views a product but doesn't add to cart. Requires the Klaviyo JS snippet installed on your Shopify theme.

  • Email 1: 4 hours after browsing — Show the viewed product with CTA
  • Email 2: 48 hours — "Still thinking about it?" + social proof

5. Win-Back Flow

Triggered when a customer hasn't purchased in 90-180 days. Attempts re-engagement before they become permanently inactive.

  • Email 1: "We miss you" — showcase new arrivals or bestsellers
  • Email 2: 7 days — "Here's a special offer for your return" (discount)
  • Email 3: 14 days — Final attempt: "Is this goodbye?"

Klaviyo SMS for Shopify: Supercharge Your Flows

SMS through Klaviyo achieves 95%+ open rates vs 20-25% for email. Adding SMS messages to your flows — particularly abandoned cart and post-purchase — can meaningfully increase revenue attributed to Klaviyo.

Setting Up SMS in Klaviyo

  1. In Klaviyo, go to Account → Settings → SMS and enable the SMS channel for your country.
  2. Add a phone number field to your signup forms and collect SMS consent explicitly (TCPA/GDPR compliance).
  3. Enable SMS consent collection at Shopify checkout under Checkout → Customer information.
  4. Add SMS message nodes to your existing email flows (e.g., an SMS 30 minutes after abandoned cart email).
SMS Compliance Note: SMS marketing is heavily regulated. Always get explicit opt-in, include an opt-out keyword (STOP) in every SMS, and respect quiet hours (no messages before 8am or after 9pm local time). Ibrar Zahoor always implements compliant SMS collection in client projects.

Revenue Attribution in Klaviyo

Klaviyo's attribution model counts revenue when a customer clicks a Klaviyo email and places a Shopify order within the attribution window (default: 5-day click, 1-day open for email; 24-hour click for SMS).

A common mistake is reporting only Klaviyo-attributed revenue as "email revenue" without considering that some of those customers may have purchased regardless. For a more accurate view, Ibrar Zahoor recommends comparing Klaviyo-attributed revenue to a baseline of organic repeat purchase rate to calculate true incremental revenue.
