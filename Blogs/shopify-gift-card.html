Shopify Gift Card Setup — Drive Sales & Acquire New Customers

Gift cards are one of Shopify's most underutilized revenue tools. They bring in immediate cash flow, acquire new customers through gifting, and drive repeat visits. A properly set up Shopify gift card program — with custom designs, balance tracking, and promotional campaigns — can become a meaningful revenue stream.

Shopify's Native Gift Card Features

Shopify has solid native gift card functionality built directly into the platform — no app required for the core experience. Understanding what's available out of the box helps you decide where third-party apps add genuine value versus unnecessary complexity.

How Shopify Gift Cards Work

A Shopify gift card is essentially a unique alphanumeric code stored against a balance in your store's system. When a customer purchases a gift card, the order is fulfilled immediately — the gift card code is emailed to the recipient (or purchaser, for self-gifting). The code is redeemable at checkout, where Shopify automatically checks and deducts the balance. Remaining balances are tracked and customers can check their balance through your store or order confirmation emails.

POS Compatibility

Gift cards issued through Shopify are immediately compatible with Shopify POS — no additional setup needed. Staff can scan or manually enter the gift card code at the POS terminal and the balance is applied directly to the in-store transaction. This makes gift cards an excellent cross-channel tool for stores with both online and physical presence.

Revenue Insight: On average, 60–80% of gift card value is actually redeemed — meaning 20–40% of gift card revenue is pure profit from breakage (unredeemed balance). This is recognized as revenue under accounting standards and represents a genuine financial benefit beyond just the immediate sale.

Custom Gift Card Design & Branding

The default Shopify gift card email is functional but generic. Branded gift card experiences — with seasonal designs, personalized messages, and a premium presentation — dramatically increase the appeal of gift cards as purchasable products and as gifts customers actually feel good about giving.

Custom Denominations

Shopify allows you to create gift card products with multiple variants representing different denominations — $25, $50, $100, $250. You can also create open-value gift cards where the purchaser enters their own amount, using apps like Govalo. For most stores, a range of 3–5 denominations with the middle option (e.g., $50) positioned as the default works best for maximizing average gift card order value.

Seasonal Designs & Personalized Messages

Apps like Govalo and Gift Up! allow you to upload multiple gift card designs — a Christmas design, a birthday design, a "just because" design — that purchasers can select at checkout. The ability to add a personalized video or written message turns the gift card from a transactional product into a genuine gifting experience. This differentiation commands premium pricing and drives higher gift card sales during seasonal peaks.

Branded Email Delivery

Customize the gift card delivery email in Shopify's email templates (Settings > Notifications > Gift Card Created). The email should feature your full brand identity, a clear display of the gift card amount, the unique code in large readable text, instructions for redemption, and a curated selection of products to spark inspiration for how to spend the gift.

Gift Card Marketing Campaigns

A gift card program without active promotion generates a fraction of its potential. The best-performing gift card campaigns create compelling reasons to buy a gift card beyond "I don't know what to get them."

Holiday Promotions

Run dedicated gift card promotions for Christmas, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, and Father's Day. Create seasonal landing pages with gift card bundles, gift guides, and explicit messaging positioning the gift card as the perfect last-minute gift. Send email campaigns with countdowns to last shipping dates — when physical products are no longer viable to deliver on time, gift cards become the primary conversion opportunity and email urgency drives volume.

Incentive Programs

The "give $50, get $10" incentive — where purchasing a $50 gift card earns the buyer a $10 gift card for themselves — is one of the highest-ROI promotional mechanics in ecommerce. It simultaneously drives gift card purchases, acquires a new customer (the recipient), and brings the buyer back into your store for their own $10 credit. Configure this using Shopify's automatic discounts or a loyalty app that issues bonus store credit with qualifying gift card purchases.

B2B Bulk Gift Card Orders

Corporate gifting is a massive and underserved opportunity for most Shopify stores. Companies buy gift cards in bulk for employee rewards, client appreciation, holiday gifts, and promotional giveaways. Create a dedicated B2B gift card ordering page with bulk pricing tiers, invoice payment options (Net 30), and custom branding options. Promote this through LinkedIn outreach, local business associations, and direct outreach to HR departments in your industry.

Physical Gift Cards — Setup & Fulfillment

For stores with a significant physical retail presence or that want to sell gift cards in retail partner locations, physical plastic gift cards add a premium channel. Shopify POS natively handles physical gift card scanning and balance tracking using the same underlying gift card system as digital cards.

Card Production

Physical gift cards are produced by specialized card manufacturers — companies like Plastic Printers or CardPrinting.com produce retail-quality plastic cards with magnetic stripes or barcodes. You order a batch (typically minimum 250–500 cards), specify your denomination or variable-load setup, and receive cards pre-printed with your branding. The card codes are then imported into Shopify's gift card system in bulk using the Admin API or a bulk import tool.

Barcode Setup & POS Scanning

Each physical card needs a barcode (Code 128 or QR code) that encodes the unique gift card code. When a customer presents their card at your POS, staff scan the barcode and Shopify automatically retrieves the balance and applies it to the transaction. The setup process involves ensuring your barcode format matches the code structure Shopify expects — typically a 16-character alphanumeric string.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there laws about gift card expiry dates? +
Yes. In the US, federal law (the Credit CARD Act of 2009) prohibits gift card expiry within 5 years of purchase and restricts inactivity fees. Many individual states have additional protections. In the UK, no statutory minimum applies but consumer protection guidelines are relevant. The EU has country-by-country rules. Shopify gift cards have no expiry date set by default, which is the safest approach for compliance across markets.
Can I handle bulk corporate gift card orders? +
Yes. For B2B bulk orders, use Shopify's admin bulk gift card generation feature to create multiple cards at once and deliver via CSV to the buyer. Apps like Gift Up! and Govalo support bulk ordering directly from your storefront with automated delivery. For high-volume corporate orders (500+ cards), custom development with the Shopify Admin API provides the most efficient and white-labeled buyer experience.
Can gift cards integrate with a loyalty points program? +
Yes. Loyalty apps like Smile.io and Yotpo Loyalty both support earning points on gift card purchases and allowing points to be redeemed toward gift cards. The configuration requires ensuring both apps recognize gift card product types in their earning and redemption rules. Some stores also allow loyalty points to be automatically converted to gift card credit at a defined threshold.
Can I issue a refund to a gift card instead of the original payment method? +
Yes. Shopify allows refunding any order to a new gift card rather than the original payment method. During the refund process in admin, select the option to issue store credit — Shopify generates a new gift card code for the refund amount and emails it to the customer. This is an excellent policy to keep refund value within your store as future spend rather than losing it as a cash outflow.
Which Shopify plan is needed for gift cards? +
Gift cards are available on all Shopify plans including Basic ($39/month). There is no plan tier restriction for core gift card functionality including digital delivery, balance tracking, and POS redemption. Advanced features like scheduled delivery, video messages, and custom design selection require third-party apps (Govalo, Gift Up!) which work on all plans. Bulk API generation requires Shopify Advanced or Plus for volume operations.
