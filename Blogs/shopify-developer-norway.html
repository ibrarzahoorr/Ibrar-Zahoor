Norway-Specific E-commerce Features

Vipps Payment Integration

Vipps is used by over 4 million Norwegians and is the preferred way to pay online for most age groups. Ibrar implements Vipps directly in your Shopify checkout, reducing cart abandonment and increasing conversion rates for Norwegian customers.

Norwegian Tax & Compliance

Norwegian MVA (VAT at 25%) must be correctly configured for all B2C sales. Ibrar ensures your store handles tax calculations accurately, issues correct receipts, and complies with Norwegian consumer law including mandatory return policies.

Norway has a thriving outdoor and sports retail sector. Stores built for this market include seasonal inventory management, size guides, product comparison tools, and the high-quality imagery displays Norwegian consumers expect.
