Can I Use Shopify in Pakistan?

Yes — completely. Shopify is accessible in Pakistan and works the same as anywhere else in the world for store setup, product management, and order processing. The key difference: Shopify Payments is not available in Pakistan. This means you need to integrate a third-party payment gateway to accept payments. This is straightforward and covered in detail below.

Pakistani merchants can sell:

  • Domestically — to customers in Pakistan via cash-on-delivery, JazzCash, Easypaisa, or bank transfer
  • Internationally — to customers in the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, and beyond via 2Checkout, PayPal, or Stripe

International Shipping from Pakistan

For export-focused Shopify stores in Pakistan:

  • DHL Express — fastest, most reliable, premium price ($20–$60 for small packages to USA/UK)
  • FedEx International — competitive, excellent tracking
  • TCS International — lower cost, good for UK-Pakistan corridor
  • Pakistan Post (EMS) — cheapest option but slower and less reliable tracking

When pricing your international shipping, build the courier cost into either a flat-rate shipping charge or your product price. Free international shipping (baked into the product price) dramatically increases conversion rates for international customers.

Tax and Legal Considerations for Pakistan Shopify Stores

Running a legitimate e-commerce business in Pakistan requires understanding your tax obligations:

  • Sales Tax: In Pakistan, sales tax (GST) is 17% at the federal level for most goods. If you sell domestically, you may need to register with FBR for sales tax collection once your annual turnover exceeds the registration threshold.
  • Income Tax: E-commerce income is taxable under Pakistan's Income Tax Ordinance. If you are a freelancer or individual seller, file under the sole proprietor category. If you have registered a business, file as a company.
  • Export Exemptions: Sales to international customers (exports) are generally zero-rated for GST purposes in Pakistan — a significant advantage for export-focused stores.
  • FBR Registration: Register your business with FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) and obtain an NTN (National Tax Number) for formal business operations. This is also required to open a business bank account.
Pro Tip: Register your business early — even as a sole proprietor. Many international payment gateways, including 2Checkout and PayPal Business, require proof of business registration and a verified bank account. Starting the process before you launch saves delays later.

Setting Up Your Shopify Store for Pakistan-Specific Success

Currency Settings

If selling domestically, set your store currency to PKR (Pakistani Rupee). If selling internationally, set to USD or GBP and let Shopify's multi-currency feature handle conversions. The free Shopify Markets feature handles currency switching and localization automatically.

Language and Localization

Consider a bilingual store (English + Urdu) if targeting domestic customers. Shopify's Languages feature allows you to create Urdu translations of your store content. Urdu language support can significantly increase trust and conversion with non-English-speaking Pakistani customers.

Mobile-First Design

In Pakistan, over 85% of internet access is via mobile phone. Your Shopify store must be fully mobile-optimized — fast loading on 3G/4G connections, touch-friendly, and streamlined checkout. Avoid heavy images and JavaScript-heavy themes.
