Technical SEO vs Content SEO — What's the Difference?

Many store owners confuse content SEO (writing blog posts and product descriptions) with technical SEO (fixing how Google crawls and understands your site). Both matter, but technical SEO is the foundation. Without it, even the best content won't rank.

TypeWhat It CoversImpact
Technical SEOCanonical tags, sitemap, robots.txt, schema markup, site speed, duplicate URLs, crawlability, mobile-friendlinessDetermines if Google can find and index your pages

Key Insight: Shopify automatically generates both /collections/shoes and /products/nike-air URLs, but also creates /collections/shoes/products/nike-air — a duplicate that confuses Google. Canonical tag fixes alone can produce significant ranking improvements.

Shopify-Specific SEO Issues We Fix

  • Duplicate product URLs: Canonical tags on all collection-scoped product pages
  • Pagination issues: Proper rel=next/prev or canonical handling for paginated collections
  • Missing structured data: Product, BreadcrumbList, Organization, and FAQ schema added to all relevant pages
  • Sitemap optimization: Verify XML sitemap is complete and submitted to Google Search Console
  • Robots.txt errors: Ensure critical pages are not accidentally blocked from crawlers
  • Thin collection pages: Add unique copy to category pages to differentiate them from product pages
  • Image SEO: Descriptive alt text, proper file names, compressed images with captions
  • Internal linking: Build a logical site architecture that distributes link equity to important pages

Schema Markup for Shopify

Schema markup (structured data) tells Google exactly what your content is — a product, a review, a price, an event. Stores with proper schema markup often earn rich results in Google Search: star ratings, price ranges, availability badges, and FAQ dropdowns. These rich results dramatically increase click-through rates even when you're not in position one.

Ibrar implements all relevant schema types for Shopify stores:

  • Product schema with price, availability, and brand
  • AggregateRating schema from review app data
  • BreadcrumbList schema on all page types
  • Organization and LocalBusiness schema on the homepage
  • FAQPage schema on relevant content pages
