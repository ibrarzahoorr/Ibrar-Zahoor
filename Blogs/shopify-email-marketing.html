Klaviyo vs Omnisend — Which Is Right for Your Store?

Both Klaviyo and Omnisend are purpose-built for ecommerce email marketing and integrate deeply with Shopify. The right choice depends on your store's complexity, budget, and whether SMS is part of your strategy.

The 7 Must-Have Email Flows

Flows are automated email sequences triggered by specific customer behaviors. Unlike one-off campaigns, flows earn revenue every day without additional effort. Here are the seven flows every Shopify store must have before spending a dollar on advertising:

Core Revenue Flows — Setup Priority Order

  1. Welcome Series (3–5 emails) — Sent to new subscribers. Introduces brand story, bestsellers, and social proof. Avg open rate: 50–65%. Set up first — it's your largest volume trigger.
  2. Abandoned Cart (3 emails: 1hr, 24hr, 72hr) — Re-engages shoppers who added to cart but didn't purchase. Recovers 5–15% of abandoned carts. Include product images and dynamic cart contents.
  3. Browse Abandonment (2 emails) — Fires when a subscriber views a product but doesn't add to cart. Lower intent than cart abandonment but higher volume. Avg open rate: 40%.
  4. Post-Purchase Series (3–4 emails) — Builds relationship after first purchase. Includes order confirmation enhancements, delivery anticipation, product education, and cross-sell suggestions.
  5. Win-Back Flow (3 emails) — Targets subscribers who haven't purchased or opened emails in 90–180 days. Reactivate or suppress to protect deliverability.
  6. VIP Flow — Triggered when a customer reaches a high spend threshold. Exclusive offers, early access, and personalized acknowledgment drive loyalty.
  7. Review Request (2 emails) — Sent 7–14 days after delivery. Feeds your review app (Judge.me, Okendo, Yotpo) and generates social proof for your product pages.

Technical Integration Setup

A properly configured Klaviyo integration goes far beyond installing the Shopify app. The depth of your integration determines the precision of your segmentation and the accuracy of your revenue attribution.

Event Tracking Configuration

Shopify's native Klaviyo integration tracks: Viewed Product, Added to Cart, Started Checkout, Placed Order, Ordered Product, Fulfilled Order, Cancelled Order, and Refunded Order. Each event passes rich properties — product ID, SKU, price, collection, variant — that enable granular segmentation: "Viewed running shoes, did not purchase, lifetime spend over $200."

Custom Properties & Segments

Beyond standard events, I configure custom profile properties synced from Shopify metafields and customer tags: subscription tier, preferred product category, wholesale status, loyalty points balance. These properties power dynamic content blocks and precise segmentation that out-of-the-box integrations can't achieve.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Klaviyo cost for Shopify stores? +
Klaviyo is free for up to 250 contacts with 500 email sends per month. Pricing scales from $20/month for 500 contacts to approximately $150/month for 10,000 contacts. Email + SMS combined plans are available at higher tiers. Klaviyo's revenue attribution reports typically show 20–30x ROI for well-configured accounts.
How long does it take to fully set up Klaviyo with Shopify? +
Basic Klaviyo integration with Shopify (app connection, historical data sync, enabling default flows) takes 3–5 days. A complete setup with branded email templates, all 7 core flows with custom logic, full segmentation, and deliverability infrastructure (SPF/DKIM/DMARC) typically takes 2–3 weeks.
Can you migrate my existing email list to Klaviyo? +
Yes. List migration involves exporting your current subscribers, cleaning for invalid addresses and spam traps, mapping custom properties to Klaviyo profile fields, and importing with correct consent flags. We also configure suppression lists from day one to protect your sender reputation.
What open rates should I expect from Shopify email flows? +
Triggered flows consistently outperform broadcast campaigns. Welcome series: 45–60% open rates. Abandoned cart emails: 35–50%. Post-purchase sequences: 40–55%. Win-back flows: 15–25%. These figures assume proper list hygiene, authenticated sending domain, and optimized subject lines.
Is Klaviyo GDPR compliant for European customers? +
Klaviyo provides GDPR compliance tools including double opt-in forms, consent timestamp recording, data deletion request automation, and a Data Processing Agreement. Your signup forms must clearly state what subscribers are opting into, and your privacy policy must reference email marketing data usage to achieve full GDPR compliance.
